Chilli Con Carne

The below is a recipe for Chilli Con Carne, a simple and warming dish.


Ingredients
  • 500g Minced Beef
  • 800g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 400g tin of Kidney Beans (drained)
  • 2 Medium Brown Onions
  • 4 Cloves of garlic
  • 3 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • Half a vegetable or beef stock (disolved into 400ml boiled water)
  • Vegetables to taste
Recipe
  1. Add some oil to the pan and warm being careful to not let it smoke
  2. Whilst the oil is heating up prepare the onions and garlic ahead of time
  3. Add the onions intially, moving them periodically as to prevent burning
  4. Whilst the onions are cooking prepare all the dry spices from above
  5. Finish the Chilli
